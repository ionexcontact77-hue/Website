01 // Executive Thesis

Scaling Electric Propulsion

Most electric propulsion systems today are designed for small satellites operating in low Earth orbit. While efficient, these platforms do not scale cleanly in thrust, thermal management, or lifetime for higher-mass orbital transfers.

i-one-x is exploring large-class gridded ion thruster architectures through computational modeling of discharge chambers, acceleration grids, and thermal behavior. This project is currently in an early research phase, with efforts focused on subsystem-level feasibility prior to hardware development.

02 // System Architecture
